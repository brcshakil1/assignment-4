Lunch Menu

Butter Chicken

Tender pieces of chicken simmered in a rich tomato-based sauce with a blend of aromatic spices and finished with cream and butter. Served with naan bread and steamed basmati rice.

Chicken kebab

Succulent pieces of marinated chicken grilled to perfection and served with a side of aromatic basmati rice and a cucumber and tomato salad.

Chicken Roast

A roasted chicken dish marinated in a blend of spices, herbs, and yogurt, served with flavorful rice and vegetables.

Vegetable Curry

A flavorful vegetarian curry dish made with seasonal vegetables and a blend of aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and turmeric.

Chicken Biriyani

Flavorful basmati rice cooked with marinated chicken and a blend of aromatic spices like saffron, cumin, coriander, and cardamom.

Dinner Menu

Daal

A simple and healthy dish made with lentils and spices, often served with rice and a variety of vegetable sides.

Prawn Curry

Another seafood favorite in Bangladeshi cuisine, prawn curry features juicy prawns cooked in a spicy tomato-based gravy

Shorshe Ilish

A traditional Bangladeshi dish featuring the popular ilish fish cooked in a mustard-based gravy with green chilies and spices.

Ruhi Fish Curry

Bangladeshi cuisine is known for its love of fish, and fish curry is a popular lunch dish that features fish cooked in a spicy and flavorful gravy.

Mutton Curry

A spicy and aromatic mutton curry dish that is slow-cooked with onions, garlic, ginger, and a blend of traditional spices.
